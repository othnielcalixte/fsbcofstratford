Our Story

Since its founding in 2002, the French Speaking Baptist Church of Connecticut first gathered in faith, guided by the vision of Rev. Dr. Emanuel St. Juste.

Today, that faithful beginning has blossomed into a vibrant presence across the state, with over two hundred members worshiping in a family of six churches.

For over 22 years, through our ministry and in accordance with our Southern Baptist Convention beliefs, we have seen countless lives touched. We are more than a place to go; we are a people committed to proclaiming Jesus Christ as Lord, and we trust in His continued blessings as we carry His name forward.
